RESOURCE TYPES

A resource is a physical material that humans need and value such
as land, air, and water. Resources are characterized as renewable or nonrenewable; a renewable resource can replenish itself at the rate
it is used, while a nonrenewable resource has a limited supply.
Renewable resources include timber, wind, and solar
while nonrenewable resources include coal and natural gas.

Earth Day
Earth Day

Earth Day is an annual celebration that honors the achievements of the environmental movement and raises awareness of the need to protect Earth’s natural resources for future generations. Earth Day is celebrated on April 22 in the United States and on either April 22 or the day the spring equinox occurs throughout the rest of the world.
